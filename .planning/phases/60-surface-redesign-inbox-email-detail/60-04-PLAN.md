<objective>
Turn the region overlays into the provenance marks they always were: colour states the tier, and
role is carried structurally.

Purpose: the overlays ARE the OCR token polygons the whole identity is derived from — 58-IDENTITY's
signature element is literally "an OCR-token-polygon-derived highlight on every extracted fact".
Today they say the wrong thing twice. They spend three hues on ROLE (a classification, which law 1
forbids and law 3 re-encodes), and they spend no colour at all on TIER (the one distinction that
earns it). Phase 59 already made this concrete rather than theoretical: `--graph-entity: var(--ink)`,
`--graph-email-component: var(--faded)`, `--graph-email: var(--pencil)` — the three "role colours"
now resolve to three greys, two of them 4.4% of lightness apart. The role coding is already dead on
screen. This plan re-encodes it.

Output: a shared `region-vocabulary.ts`, a re-encoded overlay box, and a matrix gate over the full
role x status space.
</objective>

    `kind === "type"` the label is polytoken's own word for a category — chrome, so sans. When
