<objective>
Establish the two foundation primitives every other Phase-53 surface builds on:
(1) the single shared `useIsMobileViewport()` hook (the ONLY JS mount/unmount
signal this phase introduces — CSS `md:` classes handle everything else), and
(2) the global mobile nav trigger that closes the found gap where NO
`SidebarTrigger` exists anywhere in the app today, leaving a signed-in phone
user with no way to open the app navigation at all.

Purpose: Without the hook, `/chat` and `/knowledge` (Wave 2) cannot decide when
to skip mounting their React-Flow islands. Without the nav trigger, MOBL-02's
"core flows usable on a mobile viewport" fails before any other surface matters.
Output: `use-is-mobile-viewport.ts` (+ test), and a `md:hidden` nav bar in
`layout.tsx`.
</objective>
