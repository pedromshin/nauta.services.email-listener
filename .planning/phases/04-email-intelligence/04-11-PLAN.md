Close the major segmentation-dispatch gap (UAT Gap "Auto-segment PROPOSES candidate Components"). The AnthropicSegmenter (app/infrastructure/llm/segmentation_adapter.py) and SegmenterProtocol already exist and pass unit tests, but nothing invokes them and no Components are written during ingestion. Purpose: Turn flat persisted attachments into addressable, segmented region Components (D-08/D-09) and dispatch parsers via the domain port (D-10) inside the path that real prod/staging emails flow through.
Output: ProposeRegionsUseCase + tests, ParserRegistryPort wiring into ingestion + parser dispatch over in-memory attachment bytes, container registration of the segmenter/use-case/registry callable, updated ingest + container tests.
