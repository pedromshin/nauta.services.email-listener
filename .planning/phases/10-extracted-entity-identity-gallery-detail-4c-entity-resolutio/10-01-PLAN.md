Repurpose the existing-but-empty `entity_instances` table from a Nauta-only mirror into the
cross-email identity store (D-03), and ship the two Supabase RPCs the resolution arms call
(D-07/D-12). This is the BLOCKING data-layer foundation: every later Phase-10 plan reads or writes
`entity_instances` filtered by `source='email_extracted'`, and the resolution backend (10-02) calls
the two RPCs created here.

constraint is relaxed, and a `source` discriminator exists. The gallery query (D-17) needs its index.

types regenerated, migration applied to local Postgres and verified live, PENDING DEPLOY recorded.
